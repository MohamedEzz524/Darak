(01)

Darak Construction

Our ground-up construction arm builds custom residences end to end — structure, envelope, and finish delivered by a single in-house team to one exacting standard. From the first excavation to the final walkthrough, the build is never handed off.

(02)

Darak Design Studio

Interior architecture, material curation, and detailing under one roof. The studio resolves every junction and surface alongside the build, so design intent survives all the way to the last fixture — nothing is value-engineered away.

(03)

Darak Development

We acquire and develop distinctive sites across Egypt’s most sought-after waterfront and estate neighborhoods — selecting for light, position, and long-term value, then building residences designed to endure on them.
