Accessibility Statement

Darak Group

Effective Date: February 12, 2026
Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Commitment to Accessibility

Darak Group is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users,
including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive and
user-friendly online experience consistent with applicable United States federal
laws and the State of Egypt.

2. Accessibility Standards

We aim to align our website with generally recognized accessibility standards,
including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA,
to improve usability for individuals using assistive technologies such as
screen readers, magnifiers, voice recognition software, and alternative input devices.

3. Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website design,
content structure, and functionality to enhance accessibility and improve
compatibility with assistive technologies.

4. Third-Party Content

Some areas of our website may contain third-party content or links to external
websites. We cannot control the accessibility of third-party platforms,
but we encourage those providers to offer accessible digital experiences.

5. Accessibility Assistance

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website or require
assistance, we encourage you to contact us. Please provide details about
the issue encountered and the page involved so we may assist you promptly.
